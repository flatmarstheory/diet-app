📌 Basic Guidelines (Diabetes Diet)
  • 1 Have regular meals — skipping meals may be dangerous for you.
  • 2 Do not starve yourself. Choose from the free foods listed for you.
  • 3 Cereals are major sources of carbohydrates and should be consumed in prescribed amounts.
  • 4 Whole pulses and legumes like rajmah, chana, soyabean, rongi etc are rich in fiber — use them regularly.
  • 5 Mild and regular exercise is good for you.
💪 Guidelines (High Fibre + High Protein Diet)
  • 1 Eat on same schedule each day and on time.
  • 2 Eat slowly, chew well and never take less than 20 minutes to finish a meal.
  • 3 High fibre foods: green leafy vegetables, fresh salads, fresh fruits, whole pulses with husk, oats, dalia.
  • 4 High protein foods: milk, paneer, sprouts, roasted chana, soyabean, besan, pulses, lassi, chicken, fish, egg white.
  • 5 Plenty of fluids: coconut water, lemon water, soups, lassi, water. Water intake at least 10–12 glasses.
  • 6 Fat (oil) content should not be more than 3–4 tsp/day for cooking.
